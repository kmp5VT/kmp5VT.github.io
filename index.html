Biography

I am a theoretical chemist with a strong background in advanced tensor algebra, quantum mechanics, electronic stucture theory and optimized high performance computer modeling


My career goals are to find robust applications of novel mathematics and to create tools to solve complex problems using high performance, heterogenous computer architectures and novel dis algorithms.


I am interested in:
  • Quantum mechanics and electronic structure theory.
  • Higher order tensor albegra and machine learning.
  • Solving multilinear and partial differential equations.
  • High performance parallel computer modeling
  • Efficient algorithm development on heterogenous computer systems

    • Recent work

    Introduction of the canonical polyadic (CP) decomposition to approximate tensor networks in electronic structure methods can a significant effect on the accuracy of the methods. Using the mathematical idea of a taylor expansion, we can find a "robust" formulation of these approximated tensor networks and significnatly reduce the cost of electronic structure theory methods.
